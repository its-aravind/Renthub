FAQ

How to cancel booking?

To cancel a booking, log in to your account, navigate to "My Bookings," select the booking you wish to cancel, and follow the provided cancellation option. Be sure to review the cancellation policy for any associated fees or refund details before confirming the cancellation.

When will I get the possession?

The possession date for a property can vary depending on the specific listing and terms negotiated with the property owner. To find out the expected possession date for a property you're interested in, please contact the property owner or the listing agent directly for precise information.

Where can I pay the rent?

We've partnered with PayPal to offer you a secure and convenient way to pay your rent through our website. Simply choose the PayPal payment option during checkout to complete your rent transaction effortlessly.

How to contact with the buyers?

To contact buyers, simply click on the "Contact Seller" or "Contact Buyer" button within the listing, compose your message, and send it through our messaging system. You can then continue the conversation with the buyer through our platform.

Why my listing not showing up?

There could be several reasons why your listing is not showing up. Please ensure that you have completed all the required information accurately, including property details and pricing, and that your listing complies with our platform's policies. Additionally, check if there's a review or approval process in place, and if your listing meets these criteria, it should be visible to users shortly. If the issue persists, contact our support team for further assistance.

How to promote my listing?

To promote your listing on our platform, you can utilize our premium listing options for enhanced visibility. Ensure your listing is complete with high-quality photos and detailed descriptions. Additionally, consider sharing it on your social media profiles and relevant real estate communities to reach a wider audience.
